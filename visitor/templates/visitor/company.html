Abhasa Financial Services originated from the experience and know-how of professionals in the field of finance and technology. Based in Ébène, the new financial hub of Mauritius, we act as a strategic financial business partner. We focus on client solutions with a view of meeting your business requirements: from the simplest to the most complex ones.

We are a one-stop-shop for all SME in terms of accountancy, taxation and finance requirements. We offer a full scope of services and comprehensive support on all strategic and operational aspects of your business.

Mission Statement

Our mission in today’s rapidly changing business environment is focused mainly in delivering the highest business standards to our clients and to add value to your needs in various ways to meet the Global challenges of tomorrow. We aim at bringing value to our clients and believe firmly in long-term relationships and take personal ownership in helping find answers to questions and provide real financial and non-financial solutions for our highly esteemed clients.
